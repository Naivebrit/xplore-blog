  • Post 3 - 9/6/17

    Working on Track Layout

    Nick, Dan, Adam, and Celeste reviewed the coaster track layouts and ironed out some of the details. Decisions were made about how the coaster is going to move on the track as well as the type of material that the track is going to be made out of. 3-D printed prototypes were made to assist in the visual conceptualization of the track. The height and length of the roller coaster is to be determined, but the constraints from how the product will be shipped give a better picture of how the coaster will come together.
